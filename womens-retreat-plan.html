Role Definitions/Expectations

Setup

Men will ensure the retreat is ready for the women. This will include:

  • Making sure the retreat space is presentable, clean, and ready for use
  • Ensuring napkins, cups, silverware, garbage bags, plates, bowls are available
  • Activities are available for the women - board games, puzzles, more
  • A variety of snacks are available


Meal Planner

Men will help prepare the breakfast, lunch, and dinner menus for the entirety of the retreat. This includes:

  • Friday dinner
  • Saturday breakfast
  • Saturday lunch
  • Saturday dinner
  • Sunday breakfast


Cook

Men will ensure the women have adequate meals. This will include:

  • Ensuring napkins, cups, silverware, garbage bags, plates, bowls supplies are well stocked
  • The food is delivered or prepared
  • The women are served food
  • Removing any meal preparations items before leaving
  • Snack supplies are well stocked


Cleanup

Men will clean up the retreat before the 11am checkout Sunday morning. This will include:

  • Cleaning up the retreat
  • Removing all garbage
  • Coordinating the return of any board games/puzzles
  • Returning leftover snack food to the church (as applicable)
