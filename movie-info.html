Family Christmas Movies


The Christmas Chronicles

When: December 6th (Friday) @ 6:30pm

The Christmas Chronicles is a holiday film that centers on siblings Kate and Teddy Pierce. On Christmas Eve, they attempt to catch Santa Claus on camera, but their plan leads to an unexpected adventure. After they accidentally cause Santa's sleigh to crash, they team up with him to save Christmas. The film features Kurt Russell as a lively and unconventional Santa Claus, and the trio embarks on a whirlwind journey to deliver presents, fix the sleigh, and restore the holiday spirit before time runs out. Filled with humor, excitement, and festive cheer, the movie highlights themes of family, redemption, and the magic of Christmas. Movie Trailer


The Muppet Christmas Carol

When: December 14th (Saturday) @ 6:30pm

The Muppet Christmas Carol is a 1992 musical film adaptation of Charles Dickens' classic novel "A Christmas Carol," featuring the beloved Muppets. The movie stars Michael Caine as the miserly Ebenezer Scrooge, who is visited by the ghosts of Christmas Past, Present, and Yet to Come on Christmas Eve. The Muppets take on various roles, with Kermit the Frog as Bob Cratchit, Miss Piggy as Emily Cratchit, and Gonzo narrating the story as Charles Dickens himself. Through these supernatural visits, Scrooge learns about the error of his ways and finds redemption, transforming into a kinder, more generous person. The film blends humor, heart, and memorable musical numbers, making it a festive and entertaining retelling of the timeless tale. Movie Trailer


Miracle on 31st Street

When: December 16th (Monday) @ 6:30pm

"Miracle on 34th Street" (1994) is a holiday film that reimagines the 1947 classic. It stars Richard Attenborough as Kris Kringle, a kind-hearted old man who is hired to play Santa Claus at Cole's Department Store in New York City. When Kringle claims to be the real Santa Claus, his sanity is questioned, leading to a court case to determine his authenticity. A young lawyer, Bryan Bedford, and a skeptical single mother, Dorey Walker, along with her daughter Susan, become involved in the case. Throughout the trial, Kris's genuine belief in the spirit of Christmas inspires those around him. The film explores themes of faith, kindness, and the magic of the holiday season, ultimately affirming that belief in Santa Claus and the joy of Christmas can bring people together. Movie Trailer
