Halloween Movie Marathon


Double Feature!

When: October 27th (Friday) @ 6:30pm

Yes, a Luther documentary to finish out the Halloween Movie marathon. October 31st is Reformation Day. According to legend, on October 31, 1517, Martin Luther nailed his 95 Theses on the door of the All Saints' Church in Wittenburg further changing the course of Christianity.

Some of the kids may not be interested much in a Martin Luther documentary, so, we'll also have Wallace & Grommit: The Curse of the Were-Rabbit playing downstairs.
